About The Program

The GitHub Externship is a 90-day fellowship program for third or pre-final year students of GitHub Campus Partner schools. The initiative provides hands-on learning with practical and on ground experience. It's collaborative, remote, and happens under the guidance of expert mentors. Students get to select from a vast variety of Open Source projects listed down by different companies.. They get paired with a mentor from participating organizations who will handhold them and review their work on a regular basis. Companies reviews the performance regularly and also provideu a monthly stipend.

Benefits For Students

  • Hands on Experience
  • Opportunity to learn as you work on real world technology problems.
  • Mentorship
  • Learn best practices under the guidance of technology leaders from the industry.
  • Real World Experience
  • Live projects to showcase on your resume.
  • Stipend
  • Attractive stipends for the students by the project organization
